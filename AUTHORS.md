# Authors and attribution

## Genesis initiator

**Horus** initiated the History Anchor Protocol v1.0.0 specification and reference implementation.

This attribution grants no protocol ownership, administrator status, upgrade key, truth authority, or right to prevent forks and independent implementations.

## Contributors

Subsequent authorship is preserved through signed commits, release signatures, public proposals, and repository history. Contributors may use legal names or pseudonyms.

The protocol remains MIT licensed and ownerless regardless of who contributes to a particular implementation.
